# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

This application is used for Instagram business automation and customer communication.

## Information We Collect

The application may collect and process:

* Instagram usernames
* Instagram user IDs
* Messages sent to the business account
* Comments left on Instagram posts
* Basic interaction metadata

## How We Use Information

Collected information is used only for:

* automated replies to comments and messages
* customer support automation
* improving communication with customers
* analytics related to message interactions

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties except when required to operate Meta/Instagram integrations.

## Data Storage

Some data may be temporarily stored on secure servers for processing and automation purposes.
